If unfortunately, page get closed during transaction, What to do? How to know the status of that transaction?

Don't Worry! Just check the e-passbook in the site and if that transaction is successful, you will have a history and if that transaction is failed, you cannot have any history related to it and if money got debited, Wait for 7days to get your refund.

Is registering for Buddy Wallet free?

It's absolutely free! You need not spend a penny to become an Buddy Wallet User. Also, there are no extra charges for adding money to the wallet or for withdrawing & Money Transfers.

Is it secure to use Buddy Wallet?

It's absolutely safe unless you share your password credentials to anyone!
Every Buddy Wallet Transaction is password protected and guarded, so your mind is always at peace while making payments.

Will I be informed when my Buddy Wallet gets debited or credited?

Yes of course. We will send you notifications whenever a transaction is processed from your wallet.

Do I need to maintain a minimum balance in my Wallet?

No, You can add as much money as you wish to and use it in the way you want. After all its's yours!

Is there any limit on the amount of funds or transactions in my Wallet?

Yes! The maximum limit is Rs. 1,00,000/- per day inclusive all type of transactions like deposit, withdraw and transfer.

Is there any validation period of money in my Wallet?

No, Your money is safe and stays in your wallet for years! We will keep you notified in case you dont get logged in for a longer time.

If a transaction done through my Buddy Wallet is not successful, will my money get refunded?

Absolutely! In case of a failed transaction, your money will be refunded and automatically gets credited to your Buddy Wallet within 7days.

Is my account Information safe?

Don't worry! Buddy Wallet take your mobile number and other details for identification and correspondance and does not share any of your details with anyone. All the information stays between you and Buddy Wallet only.

Can I recharge any mobile, DTH or other bill payments with my wallet money?

No, to serve the aim of "Save Money", no commercial purposes are served through Buddy Wallet.
